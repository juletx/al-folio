Academic personal website that includes a short description, social links, biography, interests, education, skills, experience, accomplishments, projects and contact info.

Built with [Hugo](https://gohugo.io/) and [Wowchemy](https://wowchemy.com/) theme.

## Features

- Multilingual support (Basque, Spanish, English)
- Publications from BibTeX
- Project portfolio
- CV/Resume
- Contact form
